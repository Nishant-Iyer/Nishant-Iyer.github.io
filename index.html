The Long Version

I am a simple, nerdy guy who accidentally discovered that data is the world's most interesting puzzle. My background is a bit of a remix: I started in Biotechnology, wrote a dissertation that was 10% biology and 90% data obsession, and never looked back.

Today, I'm at Capital One, working in the Refinance line of business to turn massive data ecosystems into decisions that actually help customers. Before this, I was a consultant at Temple University and a Product Analytics wizard at Unilever, where I helped global stakeholders figure out where their money was going (and how to make more of it).

I speak fluent Machine Learning, Statistical Modeling, and C-Suite Strategy. Whether it's optimizing supply chains for Colgate-Palmolive or building AI-driven tools, I thrive at the intersection of "How does this work?" and "How can we make it work better?"
